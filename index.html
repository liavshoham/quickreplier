FAQ

Good to know before you install.

Does QuickReplier work on any website or app?

Yes. The hotkey types your reply into whichever text field currently has focus on your screen — a browser comment box, a desktop app, a chat window, anywhere you can place your cursor.

Are my saved replies stored online?

No. Your categories and replies are stored locally on your computer. QuickReplier doesn't upload or read that content.

What's the difference between Free and Full?

Free includes 3 categories with up to 15 replies each. Full removes those limits entirely — unlimited categories, unlimited replies in each one.

How do I upgrade to Full?

The Full version is unlocked through a license purchased inside the Microsoft Store listing for QuickReplier.

Which Windows versions are supported?

QuickReplier runs on Windows 10 and Windows 11.
